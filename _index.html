How to Find the Cheapest Fares

Securing the best price on the UK National Rail network is quick, safe, and easy. Follow these simple steps when booking:

01 Route Details

Enter your origin and destination stations. Simply start typing, and select your match from the automated list.

02 Fares & Dates

Select Single or Return. For maximum savings, filter by 'Cheapest' rather than flexible fares, and book up to 12 weeks early.

03 Class & Discounts

Choose Standard Class for the lowest price. Be sure to select your specific Railcard type from the menu to instantly slice 1/3 off the total cost.

04 Secure Booking

Click the ticket button to route directly through our secure, accredited booking channels to lock in your guaranteed fare.
