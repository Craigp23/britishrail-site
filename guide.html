🎫 Split-Ticketing Secrets

Instead of buying one expensive ticket from start to finish, splitting your journey into multiple consecutive tickets along the exact same route can drastically cut costs without forcing you to change trains.

📅 The 12-Week Window

Advance tickets usually go on sale roughly 12 weeks before departure. Setting calendar alerts for this is the single most reliable way to see the lowest tier pricing.
